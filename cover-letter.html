Ángela Graña Varela

Multilingual communication

 Who am I? 

I’m a multilingual language lover turned translator and communicator.

I’ve lived in Spain, France, Switzerland and Scotland, and spent the past eight years working remotely for a US non-profit. Thanks to such a multicultural experience, I have become particularly aware of cultural barriers and the importance of attuned messaging. At the workplace, I love making people smile, and being my colleagues’ safe space when they need a listening ear.

In my day to day, there’s rarely a dull moment. I enjoy creating things with my own hands, so I knit, crochet, and sew. My fridge safeguards my pride and joy, my sourdough starter, which I bake with weekly. I like being in nature, which motivated me to move to the countryside, where I started a food forest. I’m an avid reader, so you can always find me with a book (or three). Not least of all, what holds the most special place in my heart is music; I sing, play live, and dabble in music production.
